Why would you use the Woodpecker WordPress Plugin?

Key benefits

  • Makes it easy to get contacts from your WordPress-based website
  • You'll never lose a lead again
  • You can contact only the interested ones
  • Your leads decide to leave your data with you

Plugin features

  • Get prospects from your WordPress web page
  • You can list campaigns and prospects, and generate subscribe forms
  • Easy and accessible shortcodes
  • Manage your forms easily (note: you can use our good-looking form, but we also offer clear subscribe forms if you need them)

Woodpecker WordPress Plugin allows you to integrate your Woodpecker account with your WordPress website.

You can use it to send contact information of the prospects who filled out the form placed on your website directly into your Woodpecker campaign.
Check our guide and learn how to set it up step by step
